_Nathan1 August 1, 2016, 12:40pm #9

Client.java server sided,

getPA().sendFrame126("Players Online: "+PlayerHandler.getPlayerCount()+ " ", 640); //quest journal title

That will change the title of your quest tab to how many players are online.
If you just want them to be blank:

getPA().sendFrame126("", 7346);
