It can also be that youy isp gives you a dynmic ip, meaning that it’ll change afther a certain amout of time. You can solve this by going to www.no-ip.com and make an account. afther that register an adress as your static ip. Then download that DUS 2.2.1 client, that will automaticly update all your hosts (ips) with the dynamic ip.

So basicly this ‘host’ will redict people that want to connect to your dynamic ip.
But this is propably only for the most DLS users.

If you’re using a router, portforward it, and open all your ports (43594 in most cases) on the computer that you serve with.

You might want to try and shut down any firewalls, even though i don’t recommend it.
