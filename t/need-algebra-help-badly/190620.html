Problem #2 Solution

The length of a rectangle is 4 more than 2 times the width.
y = 2x + 4
2 times the length plus 2 times the width equals the perimeter
2y + 2x = 20

Now replace your y value in the second equation.
2y + 2x = 20
2(2x + 4) + 2x = 20
4x + 8 + 2x = 20
6x + 8 = 20
6x = 12
x = 2

Using your new x value, plug it in to your y equation. This is called substitution.
y = 2x + 4
y = 2(2) + 4
y = 4 + 4
y = 8

Check:
y = 2x + 4
8 = 2(2) + 4
8 = 4 + 4
8 = 8
Correct!

Check:
2y + 2x = 20
2(8) + 2(2) = 20
16 + 4 = 20
20 = 20
Correct!
