Muroth August 4, 2016, 10:13am #14

Download Speccy, it’s a program that’ll tell you your PC problems. If it’s not a software issue it may be a hardware issue. There could be multiple things wrong with hardware, i.e bad ram, HDD, or you may be over heating. If you’re over heating I recommend cleaning your computer out, open it up and get any dust out. Make sure it has breathing room. If your temperatures are high when you test Speccy, you may need to apply new thermal paste and/or upgrade your cooling system.
