Here dude you have to do your environmental variables:

Open Up Computer > Properties > Advanced > Environmental Variables

At the bottom add a environmental Variable with

Name: JAVA_JDK Path: C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_(PUT YOUR VERSION OF JDK)\bin

Then Make another environment variable with this

Name: JAVAHOME Path: C:\Program Files\Java\jre7\bin

Now Go to the environmental variable named Path

Add this to the end of it: ;%JAVA_JDK%;%JAVAHOME%

Now Apply It and click OK

Now Put this into your compiler when your done:

@echo off @title Compiler javac -cp . *.java pause exit

I did that and it worked for me Good Luck
