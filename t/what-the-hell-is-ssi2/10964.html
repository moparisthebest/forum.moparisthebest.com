SRL is the Mega-All-In-One-Include for RS. The best scripters have put forth their procedures and functions in there, and it INCREDIBLY simplifies your script [assuming you would otherwise make you own anti-randoms O_O] and gives it multi character ability [i.e. one guy logs off after 20 minutes, next guy comes on].
