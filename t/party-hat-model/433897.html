Textures and materials are nowhere near the same. Materials are actually used for shading, not necessarily to color the model(or at least not anymore). Textures are used to ‚Äòproject‚Äô an image onto a model. Think of Materials as the shading method(or how light reacts with the model) to the model (hence blinn, lambert, phong, anisotropic‚Ä¶) , and the texture as the detail or image projection.
