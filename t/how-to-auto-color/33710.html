The_Un-Named July 30, 2016, 1:53am #3

Well to auto-color on a specific thing, you could say move the mouse there, check up-text to be sure and use Var := GetColor(coordx, coordy); as scarscaper4life already said.

You can implement this into practically everything, roads, trees, monsters. With roads you should use a bitmap of an orignal road and use a high tolerance, then search for it in the minimap area.
