There will be the name of the shop you want to edit on the line of the items.

The item id would be, for example 4151 (Abyssal whip), then the amount will be next to it.
So an example of a shop:

shop = 1	Abyssal_Whips				2	2	4151	10

That shop will have 10 abyssal whips in it.
