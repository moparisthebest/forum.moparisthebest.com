You Must Bring 25 Unnoted Pure essence to the cosmic alter and go INSIDE of the alter. If you trade someone with black wizard robes, you will be given 7k cash and 25 noted pure ess. If you trade someone in blue wizard robes, you will receive 35 cosmic Runes and 25 noted pure ess.

[size=10pt]Free Nature Runes :[/size]
Goto the Nat altar on world 22 , Im not sure if you have to go into the altar or wait outside.
Bring 25 pure ess with you ( just bring some gp and buy from the general store there )
Then trade whoever is there , cant remember what color they wear ( sorry ) and they will give u 50 nats for your 25 rune ess.

[size=10pt]Free air runes or 2.5k:[/size]
Goto the air altar on world 16 (non members world).
You need an air talisman or tiara & 25 empty inventory spaces. Bring 25 unnoted ess your first trip. They supply ess after that.

  1. Trade one of our crafters: one in BLACK robes to get paid in cash, or one in BLUE robes to get paid in airs.
