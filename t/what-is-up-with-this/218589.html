Vesnal1 July 31, 2016, 12:06am #2

Do you have the classpaths set? And do you have jdk 6 or a earlier version, if that is the case you will need to change the

"C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_06\bin\javac.exe" -cp . *.java

to

"C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_XX\bin\javac.exe" -cp . *.java

The xx is the version you have, so if you had 06 it would be _06 or if you had 5 it would be 05
