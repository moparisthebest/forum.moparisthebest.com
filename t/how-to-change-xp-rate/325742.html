Telenim July 31, 2016, 2:02pm #2

If your experience rate is actually set to 650 then search in client.java for

TotalExp = (double)(650*hitDiff);

The number is the EXP that you gain each time you hit a 1. Make sure to change all the others, there isn’t just one.
