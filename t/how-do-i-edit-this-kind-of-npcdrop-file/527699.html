This is how it works

Npc Name
NPC ID:Rares Drop rate out of 100 ItemID:Amount dropped

And to make an item rare, put it on the bottom line.

For example

NPC Name
NPC ID:Drop Rate First item:amount dropped Second Item:amount dropped
Rare item: amount dropped Rare item:amount dropped
