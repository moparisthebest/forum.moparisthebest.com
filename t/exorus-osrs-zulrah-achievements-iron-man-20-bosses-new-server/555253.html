[COLOR="#FFFFFF"][SIZE=3]Short Intro[/SIZE][/COLOR]

Exorus is a oldschool based server with loads of content such as all working skills, 20 + bosses to kill, iron man mode for people who like the challenge. Exorus also has a completionist cape with a total of 17 Hard tasks to complete with more tasks being added frequently. Exorus is not a easy server that you can max out right away the xp rates are set at a perfect not to easy not to hard level so players do not get bored from either taking to long or being too fast.
