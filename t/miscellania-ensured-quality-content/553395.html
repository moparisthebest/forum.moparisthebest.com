KryptikRSPS August 4, 2016, 4:33pm #6

Latest updates:

  • The dark bow is now dropped by dark beasts and no longer by mithril dragons (sorry about that hiccup). They have been added to Kuradel’s task list, and can be found on the top floor of the slayer tower.
  • The slayer helmet can now be combined with ‘Hexcrest’ and ‘Focus Sight’ to create a ‘Full Slayer helmet’. It also has its actual effect now. Rather than boosting effect, it boosts damage by 12% on your current task. The regular slayer helmet only works for melee attacks, while the full slayer helmet works for all types of attacks. The hexcrest & focus sights are dropped by jungle/desert strykewyrms.
  • There is now a stardust shop. It’s held by Bob, located in the edgeville bank (next to the vote shop). From this, you may purchase XP boosting items that have been implemented as well as the inferno adze. The items are as follows:
  • Lederhosen clothing - boosts your overall experience for all skills by 2% per piece worn.
  • Magic Secateurs - 50% farming XP boost and chance of double loot from herbs.
  • Inferno adze - Has already been in-game but now has its actual effect where it can burn logs you chop at a 33% rate. Its requirement has also been lowered to 61 woodcutting/mining, and is only slightly better than the dragon hatchet/pickaxe.
  • Rigour & Augury prayers have been restricted and need to be unlocked with scrolls bought from the dungeoneering store.
  • The farseer kiteshield and the eagle-eye kiteshield have both been added to the dungeoneering store.
  • The rate at which pvp artifacts are dropped has been lowered.
  • A bug with brawlers, effecting agility and some other skills (this is what caused you to get stuck), has been fixed.
  • When something you’ve farmed has finished growing, it will now say what plant it is.
  • More fixes, tweaks and such to various things. One example is the drop rate of pvp items has been tweaked (from revenants).
