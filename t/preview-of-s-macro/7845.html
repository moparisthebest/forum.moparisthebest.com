SendText - - Sends text

Click(true - - LeftClicks
Click(false) - - RightClicks
DoubleClick - - Double Clicks

Writeln - - Writes text into the small textbox
ClickAt (x, y, false) - - Right clicks at x, y
ClickAtSelWnd (x, y, false) - - Right clicks at x, y in selected window
ClickAt (x, y, true) - - Left clicks at x, y
ClickAtSelWnd (x, y, true) - - Left clicks at x, y in selected window
FindColor - - Finds a color in selected window usage: FindColor 0 etc.
