What are switch statements?

Whats the difference in using if-else statements and switch statements? If-else statements can mainly be used to make conclusions or resolutions(decisions) based or organised on ranges of conditions or values(integers), however switch statements on the other hand are used to make conclusions or resolutions(decisions) on a single integer or an enumerated value.
