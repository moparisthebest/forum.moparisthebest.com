This is the download section where you can download other peoples sources and clients. You can also download some helpful programs. http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/board,49.0.html

A compiler is basically a batch or .bat notepad program that tells you if you did anything wrong with your coding, and it also saves everything from your .java files into your .class files.

If you use someone elsesâ€™ source and client, you should not have to know much Java, just change the titles and everything. If you plan on making major changes to your server, you will have to know some Java.

There are plenty of versions of RuneScape Sources like 317, 508, 525 but trust me, you want to start off with 317s as they are the easiest to code and understand.

The source is basically what your server will look like internally, your client is what you play the server on. It is what determines what your items, monsters, and objects look like. If you have a 317 source, the best thing to do would be to have a 317 client.

Sprites are your GUI basically. It is what your client looks like. They have your backbases, your invback, your mapback, etc. You shouldnâ€™t have to do much with these.

Your models are the custom items and NPCs you add and is client sided. If you want to add an Item or NPC, there is a Model section for that on the forums and there is a wide variety to choose from. Every item should have 2 models, the drop model which is what it looks like on the ground, and the inventory model. When you add the 2 model files into your model folder, you should have to go into Class8.java and add the integer, or Class5.java for NPCs.

The Client has the same basic idea, when you change something, you need to compile, or save it.

I hope this summed up the basic idea for you. Just remember, Ctrl + F is your friend.
