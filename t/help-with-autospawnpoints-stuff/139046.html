ItemHandler.addItem(1351, 2998, 3381, 1, ItemHandler.globalItemController, false);

1351 - The item identification.
2998 - The coordinate x.
3381 - The coordinate y.
1 - The amount of the item.

You can change the item identification and the coordinates. If you wanted to spawn money, you would use this code.

ItemHandler.addItem(0995, 0000, 0000, 1000, ItemHandler.globalItemController, false);
