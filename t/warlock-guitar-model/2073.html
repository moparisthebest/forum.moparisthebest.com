I used to do that, building things with primitives and moving some vertices around to get what I wanted. However I've found out the huge importance of quads and clean models, and have been working on that.

I plan on texturing it, but I don't know which way to go.

I can UV map it, but I suck at those.
I can use Selection modifiers and apply materials to different polys to get the effect I want.
Or I could use C4D's great module BodyPaint. It makes the UVs, and while you're modeling, you simply select paintbrush and stuff and paint the colors/textures/things onto the models. Quite nifty.
