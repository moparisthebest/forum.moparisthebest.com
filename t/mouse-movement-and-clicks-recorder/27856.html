Nothing special, it just records mouse movement and clicks. You can replay at different speed. The replay is slightly randomized each time.
USAGE:
ctrl + 0 to ctrl + 9 - record, press ctrl + ` to stop recording;
0 to 9 - replay what you have recorded;
(this means u can have up to 10 different recorded mouse actionsets)
ctrl +s/ctrl + o - to save/load first mouse actionset to file;
alt + p - pause;
alt + r - resume;
ctrl + q - terminate script;

Can be used for stuff like crafting, making arrows, auto-buying etc. Not much of a macroing, still saves from some clicking-finger pain.

Comments are welcome!

Morgoth July 30, 2016, 2:04am #6
  1. Open it it SCAR.
  2. Select a client window.
  3. Press F9.
  4. Press ctrl + 1.
  5. Move the mouse around for a while, click some times.
  6. Press ctrl + `;
  7. Press 1 and mouse will move and click just the way you moved and clicked it while doing 5), only with slight path randomization.
