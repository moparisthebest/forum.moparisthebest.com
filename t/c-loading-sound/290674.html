Jython July 31, 2016, 9:30am #10 
dbLoadSound("somefile.wav", 0);

This’ll load the WAV file somefile.wav and bind it to sound ID 0.
To play it,

dbPlaySound(0);

or if you want to loop it,

dbLoopSound(0);

and, to stop it,

dbStopSound(0)[br][br][size=1]Posted on: July 24, 2009, 03:04:23 pm[/size][hr]Pausing is done via, you guessed it,

dbPauseSound(0);

and to resume paused sound, do

dbResumeSound(0);
