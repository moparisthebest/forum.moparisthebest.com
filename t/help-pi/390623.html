First you need to make sure the items you want to change are in your item.cfg.

This is the layout:


item = <ITEM ID> <Name> <Description> <Low_alch> <Shop Price> <High_Alch> <Combat boosting stats>

So you need to change the 2nd number after the description…REMEMBER TO USE TABS NOT SPACES!
