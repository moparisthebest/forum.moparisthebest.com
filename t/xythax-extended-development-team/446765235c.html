Xythax is a RuneScape© Free-2-Play remake. We are currently in the ALPHA development testing stage, and will remain in this for the next few weeks. We currently have 4 skills implemented and perfect combat complete. Unlike a lot of servers these days, Xythax isn't out there to take your money then shut down, we are here for a long, long time.

Here at Xythax, our staff team are the best of the best. We have the best moderation team a server has seen, these guys do their job perfectly and have as much fun as any other players our staff ranks also aren't like other servers, we run ours almost exactly like RuneScape, with a few alterations of course. Our staff ranks are as follows - Owner, the owner of the server, the one and only, me. Developer, our development team, you must be exceptionally good with JAVA programming to get into this team. Head Moderator, the highest staff rank a player can get to, they are in control of all moderators. Global Moderator, the rank given to users who moderate both the forums and the game. Junior Moderator, the rank given to users who moderate either the forums or the game. There's also no commands in Xythax, everything is assigned to a button, just like RuneScape.
