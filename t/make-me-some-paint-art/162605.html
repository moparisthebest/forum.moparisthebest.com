Now try to follow this image

X1 To X2 Will make the diagonal from Bottom Right to Top Left
Y1 To Y2 Will make the diagonal from Bottom Left to Top Right

These Create the square that your NPC will be walking, without always making the same course, he will walk randomly but always inside that square.
Remember to always mark the territory you want him to walk on:
First comes X1 - The Bottom Right Piece of the area
Them Comes X2 - The Top Left Part of the Area
After Comes Y1 - The Bottom Left part of the Area
After Comes Y2 - The Top Right part of the area.
Now, you have a fully created area ( A square in this tut ) that the NPC will be walking in!
