Well it looks like you just threw a whole bunch of primitives together, Here are some tips. Get a reference image and work off of it. Try trace modeling, This will eventually lead into freehand modeling witch is actually fun to do. But try making simple shapes. A cylinder with a ball on the end (This can make a hilt). Make a square, Then stretch the sides to make a blade (This can make a blade‚Ä¶) Then join the two together to make a simple sword. Now that you have mastered the basic‚Äôs of prim‚Äôs. Time to get into detail, Try to menipulate the primitive by adding section to move around. This can really be usefull in the future, But do not try to keep using the same method of modeling because after a while you‚Äôre models will grow old.

Here is a list of models you can make to get you started,

A sword.
A shield.
A NPC (okay not that yet).
